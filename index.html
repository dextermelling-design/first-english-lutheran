Welcome to FELC

For more than 140 years, First English has been part of the Richmond community — founded in 1884 so that worship could be offered in English, and still gathering people of every generation around the good news of Jesus Christ.

Our motto is simple and bold: Reaching, Growing, Loving. We work to be the hands and feet of Christ in our city, grow deeper in faith, and love other people well.
