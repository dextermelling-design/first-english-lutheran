Our mission

First English Lutheran Church exists to proclaim the Gospel of Jesus Christ, gather people into a caring community of faith, and equip disciples for lives of worship, learning, and service.

What we believe

We are a Lutheran congregation in the tradition of the Reformation. We trust the promises of God revealed in Scripture, receive grace through Word and Sacrament, and confess the creeds of the Church universal.

  • Grace alone — we are saved by God’s free gift, not by our merit.
  • Faith alone — we receive that gift through faith in Jesus Christ.
  • Scripture alone — the Bible is the authoritative witness to God’s Word.
