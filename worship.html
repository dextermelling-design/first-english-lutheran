Word & Sacrament

Worship with us

Traditional and contemporary services every Sunday in Richmond.

Sanctuary

8:00 AM

Traditional Lutheran worship with liturgy, hymns, and Holy Communion.

Choir & handbells often serve this service.

Fellowship Worship Center

9:15 AM

Contemporary worship with the Praise Team and instrumental Christian music.

A welcoming, engaged style of gathering around Word & Sacrament.

Sanctuary

10:30 AM

Traditional worship in the sanctuary — hymns, organ, and the gathered congregation.

Choir & handbells often serve this service.

What to expect

  • Warm greeters and a printed bulletin
  • Singing, prayer, Scripture, and a message
  • Holy Communion as part of our worship life
  • Active participation — responsive readings and shared prayer
  • Coffee and donuts served 8:15–9:15 AM
  • Come as you are — everyone is welcome

Music

Music is central to life at FELC. The traditional choir and handbells lead at the 8:00 and 10:30 services (practice Wednesdays). The Praise Team leads contemporary music at 9:15. Organ and additional musicians enrich worship throughout the year.

Live stream

When you cannot be with us in person, join online. Our media ministry streams and archives services so you can worship from home.

Holy seasons

Advent, Christmas, Lent, Holy Week, and Easter shape our year with special services, music, and opportunities for devotion. Watch the church calendar and announcements for dates each season.

For baptisms, weddings, funerals, or pastoral care, please contact the church office or Pastor Chip.
