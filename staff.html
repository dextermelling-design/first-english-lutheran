Lead Pastor

Chip Belanga

Pastor Chip has served at First English Lutheran Church since June 2016. His wife, Katie, is a teacher in the Richmond Community Schools, and they have three children: Logan, Gavin, and Addison. Pastor Chip graduated from Trinity Lutheran Seminary in Columbus, OH, and served a congregation in Ohio before coming to FELC. He is passionate about sharing the love of Jesus with ALL people and introducing new believers to an amazing God.
